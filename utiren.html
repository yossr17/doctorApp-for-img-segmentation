Endometrial cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the uterus

Endometrial cancer begins in the layer of cells that form the lining (endometrium) of the uterus. Endometrial cancer is sometimes called uterine cancer.
Endometrial cancer is often detected at an early stage because it frequently produces abnormal vaginal bleeding.
